Reefs White Water Rafting

We exist to reconnect people with the raw power and beauty of nature through unforgettable whitewater rafting adventures. Our mission is to deliver world-class whitewater rafting experiences that prioritize safety, fun, and environmental respect. We believe the river is a teacher — it builds courage, demands respect, and rewards teamwork.

Founded in 2012 by a group of passionate river guides and adventure enthusiasts, Reefs White Water Rafting was born on the banks of the roaring Niger in Nigeria.

History

What started as a weekend hobby among friends who loved chasing rapids quickly grew into a mission to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with others.
